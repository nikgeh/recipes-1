# Tiramisu

-- Ingredients

* Mascarpone Cheese
* Lady Fingers
* Marsala
* Whipped Cream
* Espresso
* Rum


-- Directions

* Whip mascarpone cheese, cream in a bowl till soft peaks
* Fold in 1/3 cup coffee
* Dip lady fingers in coffee
* Spread thin layer of mixture in tray
* Lay one layer of fingers in a baking tray
